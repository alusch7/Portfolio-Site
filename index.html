About Me

Hi! I'm Alden and this is my personal portfolio website that I put together using HTML, CSS (Bootstrap), SASS, and the tiniest amount of JavaScript. I plan to keep it updated with my interests, projects I've been working on, and my experience -- and I'm also trying to practice a little bit of web development along the way.

I am an Mechatronics Engineering student currently pursing a Bachelor's at McMaster University, and I love R&D, coding, and any sort of engineering that presents a technological challenge. Last year, I worked at Microbix Biosystems, helping them implement new robotic production systems, like pipetting robots, automatic fluidic setups, and custom (self-designed!) monitoring equiment. Before that, I worked at an automotive materials lab at McMaster, helping to research corrosion inhibition methods for magnesium alloys.

Outside of school, I love to bike (feel free to follow me on Strava!), listen to music, and work on small electronics or programming projects (something I really wish I had more time for). Feel free to check some of them out below! Also, please enjoy my super flattering headshot.
